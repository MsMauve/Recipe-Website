Teriyaki Chicken

Description

Once I had a couple solid pasta dishes under my belt, I figured it was time to expand my palette, and my partners' alongisde me! This tangy chicken goes best with sticky white rice and your fave veggie!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Brease: 2 lbs., cut
  • Sesame Seed Oil: 1 tbsp.
  • Garlic: 2 cloves, peeled and crushed
  • Ginger: 1 tsp., pureed
  • Cornflour: 2 tsp
  • Soy Sauce: 100mL
  • White Wine Vinegar: 2 tbsp
  • Honey: 3 tbsp

Steps

  1. Mix the teriyaki sauce ingredients in a small bowl. Begin with the cornflour and gradually add the soy sauce, whisking so there are no lumps. Add the rest of the sauce ingredients.
  2. Heat the oil in a frying pan until very hot.
  3. Add the chicken and cook for about 10-15 minutes until the chicken is almost cooked through.
  4. Add the garlic and ginger and cook for 3 or 4 more minutes until softened.
  5. Add the teriyaki sauce ingredients and cook for about 5 minutes until everything is well coated and thickened.
  6. While the chicken is cooking, I cook some rice in the bottom of a steamer pan and then add some green vegetables to the steamer part on top for the last 5 minutes. (You can mix the cooked broccoli into the sauce and chicken just before serving if you'd like it coated in sauce.)
